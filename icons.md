## Free(ish) Icon, SVG, etc Libraries


- [Vecteezy](https://www.vecteezy.com): Trustpilot, TechRadar, and G2 rate highly.

- [Flaticon](https://www.flaticon.com): Offers a large collection of free icons in various formats including SVG, PSD, PNG, and more. They also provide icons in different styles and for various themes.

- [SVG Repo](https://www.svgrepo.com): A great source for free SVG files that you can use for commercial and personal projects without attribution. They offer a wide range of categories and styles.

- [Iconfinder](https://www.iconfinder.com): Provides both free and premium icons. You can filter your search to show only free icons, which are available in multiple formats.

- [Icons8](https://www.icons8.com): Known for high-quality icons and also offers photos, illustrations, and music. While many assets are free, some require attribution or a subscription for higher quality downloads.

- [The Noun Project](https://thenounproject.com): Features a massive collection of icons from various artists. You can use icons for free with attribution, or subscribe for royalty-free access.

- [Pixabay](https://www.pixabay.com): While primarily known for free stock photos, Pixabay also offers a good selection of vector graphics and illustrations, all free for commercial use with no attribution required.

- [Freepik](https://www.freepik.com): Offers a diverse selection of pirate ship icons. You can choose from different styles like black filled, hand-drawn, lineal color, and others. These icons are available in free SVG format, which is perfect for scalable and high-quality web graphics.
